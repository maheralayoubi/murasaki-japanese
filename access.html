Location

New Toranomon Building 3F, 2-5-5, Toranomon, Minato-ku, Tokyo, 105-0001

1 minute walk from Toranomon Hills Station, Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line

2 minutes walk from Toranomon Station, Tokyo Metro Ginza Line

Access from the Station

  • From Toranomon Hills Station
  • From Toranomon Station

    • From A1a Exit

      • Go out A1 Exit
      • Cross the crosswalk in front and take right
      • Go across the first traffic light
      • MJI is on the 3rd floor of the white building in front

    • From A2a Exit

      • Go out A2 Exit and take left
      • Go across the first traffic light
      • MJI is on the 3rd floor of the white building in front

    • From 2a Exit

      • Go out 2a Exit and go straight
      • You will see a white buildingn on you right side before the second traffic light
      • MJI is on the 3F of the building

    • From 2b Exit

      • Go out 2b Exit and go straight
      • Turn right at the second traffic light
      • MJI is on the 3F of the white building in front
