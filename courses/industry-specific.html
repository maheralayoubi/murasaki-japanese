Lesson Levels

Legal Japanese

  • Recommended for :
    lawyers, judical scrieveners, workers at law firms

The goal of Legal Japanese Lessons is to understand Japanese laws and be able to make Japanese legal documents. MJI has been offering Japanese lessons to lawyers and workers in law firms for over 10 years. Japanese terms in this field are so complicating that even the Japanese find difficult to understand. Professional teachers with expertise will explain clearly the concepts and usage of those terms. You will also learn the way of communication with your clients who are asking for legal advice.

As a result of this course, you will be able to:

  • read Japanese laws, judical precedents, and contract documents
  • make contract documents
  • make documents on intellectual property registration
  • understand fully the articles on Japanese legal issues
  • discuss on judical records and other legal issues in Japan
  • explain legal strategies to your clients
  • explain the differences between legal system in Japan and your country

Financial Japanese

  • Recommended for :
    Bankers, Accountants, Traders, Dealers

Financila Japanese lessons focus on handling financial data reported in Japanese. You will practice reading and writing financial reports, making presentations, and discussing financial affairs. If you are working in the sales department, we can design your lessons for communication with Japanese clients. Through our lessons, you will acquire keen eyes for complex financial system in Japan, changing day-by-day.

As a result of this course, you will be able to:

  • discuss Japanese economy and financial system
  • read financial reports and discuss them
  • write financial reports based on the analysis on quarterly corporate reports
  • read articles in newspapers and financial magazines
  • use statistic data effectively in presentations
  • collect and analyze information on finance and economy in Japan
  • do a presentation on your analysis to your clients

Real estate Japanese

  • Recommended for :
    Real estate agents, Land owners, Buyers

Lessons on Japanese for Real estate aim to help you learn laws and business practices of real estate industry in Japan. Our director, Kayoko Murasaki, who has a real estate license will give you clear explanations on the trends in this field including deposit key money system. Lessons mostly consists of discussion on case studies of real estate contracts, negotiation, and troubles. You can aqcuire both professional knowledge and language skills through the lessons.

As a result of this course, you will be able to:

  • read and write lease agreement and land sale contract
  • read laws on real estate
  • understand and use vocabularies and phrasese for nagotiation
  • use tools for finding real estate, apartments, and mortgages
  • analyze issues on real estate contracts
  • negotiate refunds of deposit and key money
  • introduce realestate products and services to your clients

IT Japanese

  • Recommended for :
    IT consultants, Web engineers, Web designers

In IT Japanese lessons, you will learn Japanese for programming, system design, project meetings, IT marketing, etc. Although many of the Japanese IT jargons derive form English, which are represented by katakana, it is very difficult for English speakers to understand the because of the diference of phonetic sounds. Our lessons will priorityze listening and speaking in proper pronunciation, so that you will be able to join smoothly in Japanese IT industry where communication with clients or other engineers is inevitable.

As a result of this course, you will be able to:

  • understand fully what other engineers say
  • use Japanese IT jargons
  • speak in proper accents and intonations
  • do a presentation on your design or system
  • read articles on newspapers, IT magazines, and blogs by engineers
  • write design documents in Japanese
  • exchange Japanese messages with other engineers
