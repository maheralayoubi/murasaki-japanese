Lesson Levels

Beginner Level

  • Complete beginner to JLPT N4 level
  • Study hours : 80 hours

Beginner level lessons start with basic vocabulary and grammar, with explanations in English to help you feel comfortable and learn quickly. We use practical business vocabulary and grammar, even at beginner levels, to keep your motivation high. Our original textbook depicts a variety of common business situations. We can combine language with cross-cultural experience lessons to assist you in integrating into Japanese business society.

At the end of this level, you will be able to :

  • Use 50 primary grammers
  • Use 1000-1500 vocabularies
  • Read and write 300 Kanji characters
  • Speak basic business conversation
  • Have basic formal and informal business conversations
  • Explain simple business situations
  • Greet clients and colleagues with proper formality
  • Make simple inquiries and reservations
  • Draft and present a basic business plan
  • Express your opinions on various topics with simple Japanese

Intermediate Level

  • JLPT N3 to N2 level
  • Study hours : 120 hours

Intermediate level lessons aim for you to acquire grammar and vocabulary for smooth and effective business communication at your office. Expand your range of expressions and have the ability to express your thoughts precisely. Two of our original textbooks, Functional Business Japanese and How to write emails, will help you learn very useful business set phrases and formats to write emails in proper Japanese. We can also practice business presentations and discussions to give you the confidence to participate in meetings.

At the end of this level, you will be able to :

  • Use 5000-6000 vocabularies and phrases
  • Read and write 800-1000 Kanji characters
  • Explain common business situations
  • Vary your language (formal or informal) to suit the situation
  • Read and respond to simple memos and inquiry emails
  • Engage in polite small talk with colleagues and clients
  • Write emails to arrange meetings, request documents, and acknowledge information
  • Read and comment on meeting agendas

Advanced Level

  • JLPT N2 or higher level
  • Study hours : 120 hours

MJI takes special pride in our Advanced Level classes. Our well-qualified teachers can tailor lessons to suit your industry and business for conferences, presentations, and negotiations. Our original textbook, "Case Study" will boost your communication skills and help you express your viewpoints in appropriately polite Japanese. The final goal is to communicate like native Japanese speakers, and participate actively at the forefront of the Japanese business world.

At the end of this level, you will be able to :

  • Read articles in business newspapers and trade magazines, and discuss current affairs
  • Give presentations expressing abstract ideas
  • Write complex emails for a variety of situations
  • Handle intricate negotiations
  • Catch implied meanings and carry on complex conversations
  • Draft annual reports, balance sheets, business plans and basic contracts
  • Speak smoothly with native Japanese speakers at any level of formality
